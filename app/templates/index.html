Welcome to Rare Hashes

Find and share the rarest hash patterns. Discover, contribute, and explore unique hash values with our community-driven platform.

What is a Rare Hash?

A rare hash is a hash with a repeating pattern (0 or 1) at the beginning or end. Only hashes with at least 25 repeating characters are accepted.

How to Contribute?

  1. Click Write to submit your hash.
  2. Use our Telegram Bot for automation.
  3. Send a POST request to /write with your hash data.
